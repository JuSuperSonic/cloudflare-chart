This Helm chart deploys the Cloudflare Tunnel (cloudflared) service into your Kubernetes cluster.

USAGE INSTRUCTIONS
-------------------
1. To verify the deployment:
   kubectl get pods --selector=app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "cloudflare-tunnel.name" . }}

2. To check logs for the cloudflared pod:
   kubectl logs --selector=app.kubernetes.io/name={{ include "cloudflare-tunnel.name" . }}

POST-INSTALLATION NOTES:
-------------------------
{{- if (eq .Values.configurationSource "local" ) }}
- Ensure your DNS records in Cloudflare are correctly configured to point to the tunnel : https://dash.cloudflare.com/{{ .Values.local.accountId }}
- Ensure your tunnel are correctly configured and that it indicates the status either healthy : https://one.dash.cloudflare.com/{{ .Values.local.accountId }}/networks/tunnels
{{- else }}
- Ensure your DNS records in Cloudflare are correctly configured. https://dash.cloudflare.com
- Ensure your tunnel are correctly configured and that it indicates the status either healthy : https://one.dash.cloudflare.com
{{- end }}

For more information, visit the official cloudflared documentation:
https://developers.cloudflare.com/cloudflare-one/connections/connect-apps/
